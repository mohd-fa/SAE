About

The Society of Automotive Engineers is an international organization focusing on engineers from the Automotive and Aerospace domain. The SAE Collegiate Club of Sri Chitra Thirunal College of Engineering was the first in Kerala established by Dr. U Prakash in 1996. Since then the club has remained active for the past 20+ years with more than 200 members every year.
